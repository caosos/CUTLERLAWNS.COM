# Mission

## Core platform mission

Build a full business tool for a landscaping and property-services company, not just a website.

The platform should help Cutler Lawns / Cutler Property Preservation & Landscape Services:

- get new leads;
- convert leads into estimates;
- manage customer accounts and properties;
- manage recurring maintenance customers;
- track worker and crew activity;
- verify job completion with photos, timestamps, checklists, notes, labor, and materials;
- respond to customer complaints with proof;
- issue invoices and track payment status;
- accept payments through a real payment provider in the future;
- track revenue and business performance;
- organize service history by property;
- support AI-powered yard design and project planning.

## Public mission

The public site should present a premium, commercial-first landscaping and property-services brand while still supporting residential work, winter services, calculators, quote requests, galleries, reviews, and AI Yard Builder marketing.

## Private mission

The private platform should give the owner visibility into what happened, where it happened, who did it, what was documented, what was invoiced, what was paid, and what still needs attention.

## Accountability mission

If a customer says a job was not done, the owner should be able to pull up the assigned crew, date/time, before photos, after photos, checklist, worker notes, materials used, and completion timestamp.
