# Job Proof Workflow

## Job / work-order records

Each job or work order should eventually support:

- customer;
- property;
- service type;
- scheduled date/time;
- assigned worker/crew;
- status;
- checklist;
- before photos;
- after photos;
- internal notes;
- customer-visible notes;
- materials;
- labor/time;
- invoice link;
- payment status;
- completion timestamp.

## Statuses

Possible statuses:

- requested;
- estimating;
- estimate sent;
- approved;
- scheduled;
- in progress;
- completed;
- invoiced;
- paid;
- cancelled;
- disputed.

## Dispute response

The proof workflow exists so the owner can answer questions or complaints with job records, photos, notes, material usage, labor entries, assigned crew, and completion timestamps.
