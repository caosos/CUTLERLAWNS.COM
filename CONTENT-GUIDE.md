# Content Guide

## Brand direction

Content should position Cutler Lawns / Cutler Property Preservation & Landscape Services as a premium, reliable, commercial-first landscaping and property-services company with a strong residential division.

## Public content areas

- Homepage hero and commercial-first value proposition.
- Residential division.
- Property preservation.
- Winter services.
- Service pages.
- Gallery/stories.
- Review-source cards.
- Contact and quote request forms.
- AI Yard Builder marketing section.
- Calculators.
- Project cart / estimate builder.

## Private content areas

Private content should be written for action and accountability:

- customer service requests;
- property notes;
- estimate messages;
- invoice messages;
- worker checklists;
- internal job notes;
- customer-visible job notes;
- proof-photo captions;
- admin review comments.

## Approval rules

- Customer uploads are private.
- Worker proof photos are private by default.
- Public gallery photos must be approved before display.
- Customer addresses must not be exposed publicly.
- Internal notes must stay internal unless explicitly marked customer-visible.
