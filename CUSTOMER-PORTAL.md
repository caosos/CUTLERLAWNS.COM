# Customer Portal

Customers should eventually be able to:

- create and log into an account;
- view their properties;
- request services;
- upload photos of problems or desired projects;
- view estimate requests;
- approve estimates;
- view invoices;
- pay invoices through a real payment provider;
- view job history;
- view before/after photos posted by workers when approved for customer visibility;
- message or submit notes/questions;
- request recurring service;
- view AI yard design concepts tied to their property.

Customer data should be scoped to that customer only. Customers must not see internal worker notes unless a note is explicitly marked customer-visible.
